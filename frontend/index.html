2. Tamper Protection (Distributed Chain Sync)

This node is programmed to perform a full ledger validation during chain synchronization. It verifies every block's proof-of-work, previous hash linkage, and transaction signatures. If a malicious peer tries to push a compromised or "longer" chain, the node will automatically detect the corruption and reject it to protect the decentralized consensus.
